Phase 1: Activate

In the first instance, we will guide you through the Activate Phase, which operates at both a company and Product level. The objective is to agree a Business Plan between you and your Channel Manager, including your Product focus and goals.

After the Business Plan phase, we will then work with you top down to identify and define the team support structure for each of your chosen Product pillars. Finally, in preparation for the Enable Phase, we will work with you to define a detailed Training Plan to identify who will receive training and complete certification exams.
