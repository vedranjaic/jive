The intention of the Business Plan is to define who you are, where you want to go and how we can work together to achieve our common goals

This will be defined in the following sections:

  1. Partner Profile (recent revenue with Oracle, Regional & Industry Coverage) by Product
  2. Capabilities (current & planned) by Product
  3. Growth Areas
  4. Partner Ambition (Mission Statement)
  5. Action Plan / Issues Involved

See below for details on this process and please contact your SaaS Channel Manager to define a joint Business Plan if not already started

Partner Profile

  • Recent Financials

    Identify your recent Cloud Revenue with Oracle Products

  • Regional Coverage

    Identify in which Regions / Countries you have presence with / without and OPN ID in those countries

  • Industry Coverage

    Identify the Industries in which you have coverage and/or have Package Implementation Solutions. This to be done by Product

Capacities & Capabilities

  • Overview

    In this section we will assess your current Maturity Level by reference to a number of indicators, including:

    • Current number of Consultants and Projects
    • Expected number of Projects in the near future
    • Demo Access, Reference Customers and other Indicators

  • Growth

    In this section we will jointly agree Growth areas and structure this plan with indicators such as:

    • Industry
    • Number of additional Consultants
    • Timeline

  • Ambition

    Partner to define / share their mission statement

Action Plan / Issues Involved

  • Actions

    This segment contains the actions to act on in order to achieve the desired results defined in the growth area, e.g.

    • Fixed Scope Offers (FSO's) (Packaged Solutions)
    • Grow competencies by registering for bootcamps
    • Go to market activities
    • Reference stories as pdf on partner's webpage
    • Others
