Phase 4: Handover

Having successfully implemented the first projects and having passed the relevant certification exams, you will then be able to apply to be recognized as an Oracle Specialized Partner for the Products in which you are now expert.

We will guide you through this process mainly with the support of our Partner Business Centre (PBC) Team.

We will also help you with the process of putting customer references in place and how you can leverage those within Oracle and beyond!
