Expected Outcome/Results from all the Trainings you find below

  1. SaaS Delivery Framework
  2. Cloud Scoping library/template
  3. Configuration Requirement Questionnaires
  4. Implementation Methodology and Approach adoption
  5. Project Plan Template
  6. Proposal Template
  7. Package Solutions/FSO
Training Approach
1. Training On Demand Recorded Sessions - also recommended prerequisite to maximize the benefit of the In Class Sessions

The recorded sessions below will enable partners to be able to complete the above 7 expected outcomes from the training and be able to deliver SaaS projects successfully.

The Training on demand can be done at your own pace and in no specific order based on the areas where you feel you need to expand your knowledge of SaaS Implementations.

2. In Class Training Sessions

The SaaS Implementation Best Practices in class 2 day training is a business case oriented workshop driven, which will reinforce the training on demand recorded session learning through exercises and simulations.
